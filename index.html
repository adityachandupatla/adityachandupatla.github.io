About Me

Software engineer with a demonstrated history of working in distributed systems and database industry. I bring academic expertise coupled with Industry best-practises to the table.

Currently part of Data Platform Team at Coinbase where our responsibility is to build foundational data infrastructure for the company. This involves building data access controls for our Snowflake warehouse, building Golang based microservices for data retention to adhere to compliance, developing airflow-based ETL pipelines, and much more.

In 2021, I worked as a Software Engineer at Tesla with the Ownership Experience Team based in Fremont, California. I have also interned in the same team during my final semester of college. My primary responsibilities include designing and developing features and backend systems which handle transfer of Tesla vehicles worldwide.

I have graduated with a Master’s degree in Computer Science from University of Southern California in May 2021. Some of the challenging courses at USC which have pushed me to really learn the fundamentals are: Operating Systems, Distributed Systems, Analysis of Algorithms, and Web development.

In the summer of 2020, I have interned at Hulu which is now part of The Walt Disney Company. At Hulu, I was tasked with end-to-end development of a distributed tracing service which tracks over 1 million messages in Hulu’s core Metadata ETL pipeline.

Prior to my graduate studies at USC, I worked at Teradata as a full-time software engineer, in a massively parallel and distributed systems environment. I’ve worked on a diverse set of projects including but not limited to: Integration of Tensorflow to run analytical queries within database, and accelerating aggregation queries using GPU. Long back, I have also interned at VMware and Hedgehog Lab where I worked extensively on Java Spring framework and Android App development respectively.

I have received my B.Tech degree in Computer Science from JNTUH College of Engineering, Hyderabad. I have been awarded The Best Outgoing Student Award by Prof. A. Venugopal Reddy, the Vice-chancellor of JNTUH, and the chief guest, Prof. V. Ramgopal Rao, director of IIT-Delhi, for my stellar academic performance during undergraduate.
